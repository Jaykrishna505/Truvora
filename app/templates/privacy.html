Privacy Policy

How Truvora handles platform data

Truvora stores hotel account details, guest request records, private feedback, delivery logs, payment status, and local Google review button click tracking.

Google review click tracking records that a guest clicked the Google review button. It does not confirm whether the guest submitted a Google review or what they posted on Google.

Hotels should only upload guest contact information when they have a lawful reason to contact the guest. Production deployments should use secure hosting, HTTPS, access controls, and verified email/SMS providers.

This starter privacy policy should be reviewed by a qualified attorney before public launch.
